The Team

NBC Leadership

Jeremy Dumalig

President / Head of Communications
Athletics Rep

Jeremy Dumalig created the Basketball Club in August '18 and since then has worked tirelessly to manage the club. As Head of Communications, he sent out email after email and survey after survey. Despite being vertically grounded (a.k.a short), he has defied the odds with hopes of being able to dunk. In May '18, he was elected to Student Council as Athletics Rep, running on the grounds that he would keep the gym open all year round. Jeremey is a junior and a member of the varsity basketball team.

Jack Brasesco

Spokesman

Jack Brasesco, who joined the Basketball Club back in August '18, is a committed and hardworking member. He was offered (and later accepted) the spokesman position following his standout performance at Basketball 101 Unconference, and he brought in one of the best guest speakers the world's ever seen in Secular Holiday Man. He produced one of the most popular tracks in the world as Esthappen, and he assisted with communications during the club's first year of existence. Jack is a junior and a member of the varsity basketball team.

David Shields

Marketing Director

David Shields has been a part of the Basketball Club since day one. He was promoted to marketing director following the success that is Basketball 101. He played a pivotal role in the planning of the Unconference session and since then designed the official logo and contributed to overall club popularity. Looking to the future, he is planning a wide-scale release of merchandise. He improves by 1% in hopes of playing professionally at the next level at a Division 3 institution. David is a junior and a member of the varsity basketball team.

Employees

Josh Francis

Athletic Trainer

Josh Francis earned his spot on the Basketball Club team through his all-star ability to warm the bench. This Stanford enthusiast has contributed greatly to the club’s sports medicine department, and he has recovered from an injury of his own - a wrist sprain that kept him sidelined the basketball season and allowed him to become ambidextrous. Josh is a junior and a member of the varsity basketball team.

Anjali Ramanathan

Director of Expansion

Anjali Ramanathan has been a genuine fan and believer of the Basketball Club since Basketball 101. Following the 10th grade outdoor education trip, she officially joined the team as director of expansion. Her first course of action consisted of initiating peace negotiations with Soundwaves and Spring Production, and she developed an official business proposal for club expansion. Anjali is a junior.

Ina Lalic

Chief Financial Officer

Ina Lalic, despite studying in France for the first semester of the 2017-18 school year, didn’t miss a beat, joining the Basketball Club team in late January. Her yearlong presence was felt through survey participation and email responses, and at the onset of the club’s currency release of Ƀ-Bucks, she managed club funds and organized member accounts to the best of her ability. Ina is a junior.

Meg Cozadd

Co-Graphic Designer

Meg Cozadd and her graphic design skills landed her a job as a Basketball Club employee, collaborating with Camille Grigg in what is known as the official NBC flag. Her next-level basketball skills and commitment to the sport have elevated her status from member to employee of the club, and the current leadership has no doubts that this hire will positively benefit not only the club, but the basketball community as a whole. Meg is a junior.

Camille Grigg

Co-Graphic Designer

Camille Grigg took on the role of co-graphic designer of the Basketball Club following the genius creation that is the NBC flag. A former basketball player herself, she returned in 2017 specifically for the purpose of joining and hopping on the basketball bandwagon. She plans on co-leading a rebranding campaign, one that is surely to be received with super much inertia and resistance in the local community. Camille is a junior.
